This project aims to promote intangible cultural heritage, natural heritage and tangible cultural heritage. This project will be for many, an opportunity to rediscover national cultures, to familiarize them with ancestral traditions and customs sometimes a little forgotten, to discover other cultures. To know, to understand, to share is to give students the opportunity to accept cultural diversity, in order to overcome the initial prejudices, in order to discover what brings people together. It also means cultivating respect and tolerance for European cultures, creating a sense of unity and the separation of prejudices and intolerance. This collaboration will also make it possible to fight against the dropout of school for some and the lack of motivation for others.
